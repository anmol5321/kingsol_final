Our Mission

Enabling Clean Energy Across India

To become a trusted solar energy partner for customers, businesses, project developers, installers, and communities by delivering reliable products, technical knowledge and sustainable solutions.

Our customer-first approach combines reliable product availability with deep technical expertise and a long-term commitment to sustainable value. Whether you are an installer, a developer or a business going solar, Kingsol is built to be your single most dependable solar partner.

Our Vision

A Sustainable, Solar-Powered Future

To enable, enhance and ensure clean energy possibilities across the solar value chain.

To lead the transition to renewable energy by enabling, enhancing, and ensuring clean energy possibilities across the entire solar value chain.

Our Leadership

Meet Our Leaders

person

Manish Asija

Founder Director and CEO

Manish is a professional with entrepreneurial flair and brings with him experience of seventeen years in Indian power sector. He has served in two successful energy startups in leadership capacity and with Tata Power DDL in their strategy team in past. He has served in critical business functions like formulating and executing business strategies, incubating and growing strategic initiatives, leading new business lines, delivering mission critical projects and managing complex commercial processes.

person

Anmol Sharma

Co-Founder and COO

Anmol brings over 12 years of core experience in supply chain optimization, engineering procurement, and operations within the solar sector. He oversees Kingsol's pan-India logistics network, managing our 13+ strategic stocking hubs to ensure last-mile speed, secure warehouse handling, and flawless commissioning for industrial-scale EPC projects.

person

Dr. Rajesh Iyer

Chief Technical Consultant

Rajesh is a leading voice in photovoltaic technology and grid-integration systems, with a PhD in Renewable Energy. He directs Kingsol's pre-feasibility, detailed project reports (DPRs), and lender engineering workflows, ensuring that every solar array is optimized for maximum lifetime yield and bankability.
